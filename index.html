K.A.R.D (Hangul: 카드, also stylized as KARD or K♠RD) is a South Korean co-ed group composed of four members: J.seph, B.M, Somin and Jiwoo. K.A.R.D is a new group by DSP Media – home to APRIL, A-JAX, Hur YoungJi, etc – that will primarily focus on music as artists than idols. The group will launch 3 project albums, which will all feature a special featured artist that takes on the ‘Hidden Card’. All 4 members were impressive DSP trainees who are gifted in composing, writing and choreographing. The group is gained much attention for their member SoMin, who was a part of “Baby KARA” and a former member of APRIL.

HISTORY

Pre-debut

From 2012 to 2014, member Somin was a member of DSP Media's Japanese girl group Puretty with releasing singles "Cheki☆Love" in 2012, "Shuwa Shuwa BABY" in 2013 and then disbanded in 2014 with plans for the members to re-debut into other groups. She reached the finals of Kara Project, a television programme in which seven trainees competed to become the new members of label mates girl group, Kara, where she finished at the second place. On August 24, 2015, Somin debuted as the leader of DSP Media's new girl group APRIL. She announced her departure from the group, on November 9. In August 2015, member BM was featured in Goo Ha-ra's song "La La La" on her debut extended play Alohara (Can You Feel It?), and was her dance partner for the lead single "Choco Chip Cookies".

Project single

On December 13, 2016, they released their first project single titled Oh NaNa featuring labelmate Heo Young-ji as a hidden member. They had planned to release three project single before they hold their official debut. Their follow-up project single titled Don't Recall was released on February 16, 2017. On March 21, it was confirmed that they will be holding their first American tour, Wild Kard in Chicago, Houston and Los Angeles in May 2017. The tour will also include Canada and Brazil, although the dates were not announced yet.

MEMBERS


B.M born Matthew Kim (Korean: 매튜김) or Kim Jin-seok (Korean: 김진석) or Kim Woo-jin (Korean: 김우진) on October 20, 1992 (age 24) in Los Angeles, California, USA. He represents the letter K and the King card. BM stands for Big Matt. He participated in Kpop Star and trained for 4.5 years.

J.seph born Kim Tae-hyung (Korean: 김태형) on June 21, 1992 (age 24) in Chungju, Chungcheongbuk-do, South Korea. He represents the letter A and the Ace card. He speaks English and trained for 5 years.

Jeon So-min (Korean: 전소민) born on August 22, 1996 (age 20) in Seoul, South Korea. Prior to joining the group, Somin was a Puretty and APRIL member,[1] and took part on the Kara Project where she finished in second place. She represents the letter R and the BlackJokeR.
Jeon Ji-woo (Korean: 전지우) born on October 4, 1996 (age 20) in Seoul, South Korea. She represents the letter R and the ColourJokeR. She is a former FNC Entertainment trainee.
